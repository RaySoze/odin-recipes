Lentil Soup

Most people like lentil soup for sure and there are many versions of it around the world. However, since I write the recipe, it is going to be the way I like.If you don't like it, go do your own

Just kidding. You will love it.

What you need is:

  • A glass of lentil
  • One big potato
  • Two carrots
  • One big squash
  • Salt and Peppa :)

If your ingredients are ready you can follow the steps below

  1. Peel all the vegetables
  2. Boil a full pan of water and add your lentil
  3. Chop the veggies
  4. Add chopped vegies into boiling water
  5. Put a lid on it and let it cook 15 minutes
  6. Use a blender to smash boiled lentil and veggies
  7. Pour it in a bowl
  8. Squeeze a quarter of a lemon and enjoy it
