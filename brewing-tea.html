Brewing Tea

As George Orwell stated, brewing tea is a fine craft. You are going to learn this craft today by following the steps one by one

  1. Clean your tea pot properly without any detergent. Using chemicals may create funny taste
  2. Boil 1 litre of water
  3. Pour half of glass of water on the tea leaves and let them soak
  4. Pour half of litre of water on your soaked tea leaves
  5. Wait for exactly 12 minutes
  6. Enjoy it with a nice cinnamon cookie
